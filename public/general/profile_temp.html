Research Interests

  • - Rheological modification of thermoplastic resins
    Improvement of processability and mechanical properties will be carried out by rheology modification.

  • - Material design of high-perfomance and/or functional biomass-based polymers
    Enhancement of processability and mechanical properties of biomass-based polymers will be studied by polymer blend technology.

  • - Design of Functional Polymer by Additives
    To provide a novel function by additives and fillers.

  • - Material design of self-repairing polymers
    Material design of a new type of self-repairing properties will be established.
